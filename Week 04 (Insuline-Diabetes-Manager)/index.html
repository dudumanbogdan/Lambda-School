Our Mission

Living A Healthy Life

Our number one goal with Insuline application is to keep your nutrition, and glucose levels, in order. We take the "guessing" out of the equation. It's been proven that steady glucose levels result in longer lasting energy, greater focus, and a healthier system overall. Our mission with the Insuline is to give you the power to input, and monitor, your glucose levels during all hours of the day. In doing so, we can predict your future glucose levels, so you can catch any trending low, or high, glucose levels.

How it benefits you

Putting your mind at ease

With the use of the Insuline application, we can use your glucose level inputs to give you a prediction of your future blood glucose levels.

How does this benefit you?
By having predictions of your blood glucose levels, 24 hours in advance, you have the luxury of having your mind at ease, without fretting over sudden drops, or spikes, in your glucose levels.

How does it work?

As simple as 1, 2, 3 !

The process is short, and broken down into 3 simple steps:

  1. Consistently input your glucose measurements, and the time of day the input has taken place.
  2. Input insulin dose and type.
  3. That's it! Simply log your glucose measurements, and insulin intake, and the app will take care of the rest. That way you can spend less time doing calculations, and more time enjoying your day... and food!

Note: 3 days of data input is suggested for most accurate data results. Insuline does not replace a Blood Glucose Meter - Accuracy may vary. This application is not meant to be used with real life medical issues. Application has been built as an educational project.
